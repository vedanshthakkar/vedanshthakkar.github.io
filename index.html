About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Researcher with over six years of experience training and deploying machine learning systems for real-world healthcare and industrial robotics applications. My work lives at the edge where research meets production — where a model's accuracy is measured not just in benchmarks, but in clinical outcomes and lives affected.

At the University of Minnesota's Department of Surgery, I led the development of clinical NLP systems that automatically extracted symptom information from tens of thousands of patient notes, contributing to peer-reviewed publications and clinical decision-support tools. At Neocortex Robotics, I architected multimodal RAG systems and computer vision pipelines deployed at fulfillment centers across the U.S.

I hold a Master of Science in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science from Vanderbilt University (GPA: 3.93/4.00), where I also taught Image Processing and Database Management Systems. My research interests span healthcare AI, large language models, and responsible deployment of AI in regulated environments.

Research & Projects

Clinical NLP · University of Minnesota
LLM-Based Multi-Stage Clinical NLP System for Symptom Detection & Severity Assessment
Two-stage pipeline combining fine-tuned LLaMA-3.2/3.3 models for symptom classification (present/absent/negated) across 13 symptoms, with structured LLM prompting for severity scoring. Validated on >1,400 annotated clinical notes across five corpora.
F1 = 0.98 (Seizures) F1 = 0.99 (Self-harm) LLaMA-70B PLOS ONE
Medical Imaging · University of Minnesota
Multimodal Deep Learning Diagnostic Model for Adult Rib Fractures
Late-fusion architecture combining DenseNet-121 chest radiograph features with structured EHR variables. Trained on 21,658 encounters from 35 U.S. hospitals across four major health systems. Strong internal and external generalization with demographic fairness validation.
AUC 0.90 (Internal) 21K+ Encounters 4 Health Systems
Agentic AI · Neocortex Robotics
Agentic Multimodal RAG for Image-Based Product Reasoning
Visual RAG system using LangChain and a custom CLIP-based embedding pipeline stored in PostgreSQL + PGVector. Autonomous GPT agent orchestrates perception, retrieval, and multi-step reasoning for accurate SKU classification from packaging images.
CLIP Embeddings PGVector LangChain
Computer Vision · Neocortex Robotics
Deep Learning Models for Robotic Vision Guidance
Fine-tuned Mask-RCNN, DeepLab, PointRend, and YOLO for instance segmentation on 6K manually annotated RGB and Depth images. Deployed at major fulfillment centers including PetSmart via AWS S3 and Flask API for real-time robotic pick-and-place.
YOLO · Mask-RCNN AWS S3 Production
Medical Imaging · TechMah Medical
3D Bone Reconstruction & Pose Regression
Fine-tuned U-Net variant for knee joint segmentation on ~250 radiographs. Developed a 5-layer custom CNN for 3D pose regression trained on 316K 2D projections using 4× NVIDIA V100 GPUs on GCP. Deployed at University of Tennessee hospital systems.
316K Projections GCP · 4× V100 FDA-Regulated
